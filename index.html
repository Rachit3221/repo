About

Unsent is a quiet corner of the internet for the things left unsaid. Every message here was written for someone real — a first love, a parent, a friend who drifted, a version of yourself you let go.

Messages are anonymous and shared with anyone who needs to feel less alone. No accounts, no tracking, just words that needed somewhere to go.
